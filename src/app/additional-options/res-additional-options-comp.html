What is a Rear Track Hanger Kit?

The Clopay Rear Track Hanger Kit has all the parts necessary to attach your new track to the ceiling. It proVIdes an attachment point for the door track while supporting the majority of the door weight when it is in the up position. It also helps keep the track square to the door. which is required for proper operation. If you are replacing an existing door. please inspect your rear track hangers. If they are wood, look rusted or are damaged we recommend you replace them. The Clopay Rear Track Hanger Kit will work on most residential applications where the track is not more than two feet from the ceiling. If a rear hanger is needed for distances greater than two feet. custom hangers will be required. Rear Track Hangers can be easily made with 13 gauge 1 ‘/.' x 1 V." punched angle iron available at Home Depot in the Hardware Department. Your installation manual will include a diagram to help you design your rear hanger.
